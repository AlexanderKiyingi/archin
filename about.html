Our Story

FlipAvenue Limited is an architectural practice based in Kampala, Uganda. Established in 1986, we have spent decades cutting our teeth on designing and creating buildings that are both beautiful and sustainable.
We collaborate with clients to create buildings and environments in dialogue with culture and place. Our philosophy centers on the belief that architecture has the power to shape lives and uplift communities.
From humble beginnings, we've grown to become one of the country's leading architectural practices, with a passion for innovation, sustainability, and timeless aesthetics that define every project we undertake.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that shape our approach to architecture

Our Mission
To deliver exceptional architectural solutions that blend functionality, sustainability, and aesthetic excellence. We strive to transform our clients' visions into inspiring spaces that stand the test of time while respecting the environment and cultural context.
Our Vision
To be recognized as East Africa's premier architecture firm, renowned for innovative design, sustainable practices, and client satisfaction. We envision a future where every structure we create contributes positively to its community and environment.
